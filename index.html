About

I am a recent graduate of University of Victoria’s Linguistics Master’s program focusing on mobile-assisted language learning with large language models. Some of my other research interests include Human-technology interactions and technology-mediated communication; and producing games for Indigenous language revitalization. I have knowledge in a range of software such as LaTex, Adobe Suite, Unity/Blender, with experience programming with Python, C# and Javascript/HTML/CSS. By the way, check out my awesome work.




In my spare time, I like to build things.
