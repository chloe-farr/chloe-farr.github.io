About

I am a linguist focusing on human-technology interactions and technology-mediated communication. I currently produce language learning games with Indigenous communities. I have experience programming with languages including mySQL, Python, R, PHP, and C#, and knowledge in a range of software such as LaTex, Adobe Suite, Unity/Blender.




In my spare time, I like to craft things.
